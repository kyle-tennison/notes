# 1. Colonial Period

## James Edward Oglethorpe

- Georgia Colony Founder
- Born 1696 in England to a wealthy family
- Military man from Yorkshire, studied at Oxford, fought against the turks
- Was in the British house of Commons, fought for the oppressed
	- His friend died from smallpox in prison, which caught his attention to the bad conditions. 
- Started in Savannah, originally banned slavery and granted religious freedom
- Battle of Bloody Marsh: Fought against the Spanish to protect the colony
- Envisioned Georgia as a place for the "worthy poor"
