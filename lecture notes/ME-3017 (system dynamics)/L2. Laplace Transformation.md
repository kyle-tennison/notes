### Complex Operations

Addition and subtraction work like any vectors: you just add the parts seperately. 

Multiplication works mostly unchanged:

$$\alpha(a+jb) = \alpha a + j\alpha b $$

$$(a+jb)\times(c+jd) = (ac-bd) + j(bc+ad) \because j^2=-1$$
This is standard FOIL.

Division is:

$$(a+jb) \div (c+jd) = \left(\frac{ac+bd}{c^2+d^2}\right) + j\left(\frac{bc-ad}{c^2+d^2}\right)$$
