# L2. Thermodynamics

## Energy Balance

$$\Delta E_{\rm sys} = E_{\rm in} - E_{\rm out} + E_{\rm gen}$$

At the surface that is exposed to air, you can think about the energy balance as:

$$\underbrace{\dot E_{cond}}_{\rm in} = \underbrace{\dot E_{\rm conv} + \dot E_{\rm rad}}_{\rm out}$$

Which turns out to be:

$$q''_{\rm cond} A_s = q''_{\rm conv} A_s + q''_{\rm rad}A_s$$
