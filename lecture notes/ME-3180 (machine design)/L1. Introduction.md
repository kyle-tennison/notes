The **Direct Stresses** are:
- Axial Stress, $\sigma = F/A$ (no shear)
- Direct sheer stress, $\tau = V/A$ (no bending moment)

**Torsional Stress** is:

$$\tau = \frac{T \rho}{J}$$

where $\rho$ is the radius from the central axis, $T$ is torque, and $J$ is the polar moment of inertia (I believe[^1]).



**Bending stress**


$$\sigma = \frac{Mc}{I}$$

where $M$ is the bending moment, $c$ is the distance from the bending axis, and $I$ is the second moment of area.
