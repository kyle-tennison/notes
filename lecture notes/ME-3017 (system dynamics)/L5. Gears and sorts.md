# L5. Gears and sorts

The gear ratio dictates:

$$\frac {n_1}{n_2} =\frac{r_1}{r_2}= \frac{\omega_2}{\omega_1}={}\frac{\tau_1}{\tau_2}$$

Also:

$$\frac{n_1}{n_2} = \frac{\theta_2}{\theta_1}= \frac{\dot \theta_2}{\dot \theta_1}= \frac{\ddot \theta_2}{\ddot \theta_1}$$
