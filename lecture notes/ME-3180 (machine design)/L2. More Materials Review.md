# L2. More Materials Review


### Even and Uneven Materials

**Even** material shave the same properties in tension and compression. **Uneven** materials do not.

Almost every *brittle material* is uneven. 

*In this class,* assume that all ductile materials are even. 

### Brittle and Ductile

Shigley's Mechanical Engineering Design cites about 5% as "significant strain." If a material fractures before 5% strain, it is brittle; otherwise it is ductile.

### Von Mises

$$\sigma' = \sqrt{\sigma_x^2 + \sigma_y^2 - \sigma_x\sigma_y + \tau_{xy}^2}$$

From this, the safety factor is

$$N = \frac{\sigma_{y}}{\sigma '}$$
