# Colinear, Coincident, Parallel


**Collinearity** of a set of points is the property of their lying on a single line.
- From wikipedia

**Coincident lines** refer to lines that lie on top of each other, sharing all their points.
- From Geeks for Geeks

**Parallel lines** are coplanar infinite straight lines that do not intersect at any point
- From wikipedia
